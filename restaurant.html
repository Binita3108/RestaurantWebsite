Sun & Moon
Italian Dining

SUN & MOON is built on a dream of presenting classic Italian flavours in a fresh and exciting way. It is a neighbourhood restaurant with a regularly changing, produce-led menu. Expect beautiful seasonal vegetables, perfectly grilled meat, fresh seafood and indulgent desserts, alongside expertly crafted cocktails for long sunny days in our garden terraces

Our beautiful, contemporary interior and diverse menus will set your senses alight. You may think you know Italian food but we want to show you the surface is yet to be scratched and there is so much more to offer.
Our kitchen team led by Executive Chef Paul Farag in collaboration with Head Chef Gianluca Lonati takes classic Italan flavours and playfully teases out their full potential.
